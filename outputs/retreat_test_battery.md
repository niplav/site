# Meditation Retreat Test Battery
## Pre/Post July Mahasi Noting Retreat

**Timeline:** Run full battery 2-3 times pre-retreat (≥1 week apart) to absorb practice effects, then once immediately post-retreat, optionally +2 weeks for persistence check.

**Total runtime:** ~2-2.5 hours per session (depending on which optional measures included)

## Personality & Affect

### Big Five
- **IPIP-NEO-120**
  - https://ipip.ori.org/new_ipip-50-item-scale.htm (use 120-item version)
  - ~15 minutes

### Meditation-Specific
- **FFMQ** (Five Facet Mindfulness Questionnaire)
  - 39 items, standard in contemplative research
  - https://www.midss.org/content/five-facet-mindfulness-questionnaire-ffmq
  - Download PDF for items
  - ~8 minutes

### Affect/Well-being
- **PANAS** (Positive and Negative Affect Schedule)
  - https://www.midss.org/content/positive-and-negative-affect-schedule-panas
  - 20 items, tracks emotional baseline
  - ~3 minutes

### Dissociation (Important for Mahasi)
- **DES** (Dissociative Experiences Scale)
  - https://www.midss.org/content/dissociative-experiences-scale-des
  - 28 items
  - Month-long noting can produce depersonalization/derealization — baseline data essential
  - ~8 minutes

### Optional
- **TAS-20** (Toronto Alexithymia Scale)
  - https://www.midss.org/content/toronto-alexithymia-scale-20-tas-20
  - 20 items, interoceptive awareness
  - ~5 minutes
  - Include if interested in alexithymia/body awareness changes

### Mystical/Spiritual Experience (Relevant for intensive meditation)

- **MEQ-30** (Revised Mystical Experience Questionnaire)
  - https://psychology-tools.com/test/meq-30
  - 30 items, 4 factors: Mystical, Positive Mood, Time-Space Transcendence, Ineffability
  - Standard in psychedelic/contemplative research
  - "Complete mystical experience" = ≥60% on all factors
  - ~8 minutes

- **Hood M-Scale** (Mysticism Scale)
  - 32 items (or 8-item brief version)
  - 3 factors: Introvertive mysticism, Extrovertive mysticism, Interpretation
  - Based on Stace's phenomenology of mysticism
  - Original: Hood (1975), https://www.jstor.org/stable/1384454
  - Brief version validated for shorter administration
  - ~8 minutes (full) or ~3 minutes (brief)

- **Cole's Spiritual Transformation Scale** (STS)
  - Designed for spiritual transformations following significant life events
  - 2 factors: Spiritual Growth & Spiritual Decline
  - Originally validated for cancer patients (Cole et al., 2007)
  - Applicable to transformative experiences like intensive retreats
  - High internal reliability (α = 0.98 for growth, 0.86 for decline)
  - https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/17458862/
  - ~10 minutes
  - **Note:** May need to adapt wording from "cancer diagnosis" to "retreat experience"

## Implementation Notes

### Test Order Suggestion:
1. Cognitive tests (fresh, high attention)
2. Questionnaires (less demanding)
3. Mystical experience measures (if using, do at end)

### Baseline Protocol:
- Run 2-3 times pre-retreat, ≥1 week spacing
- Same time of day for all sessions
- Controlled: caffeine intake, sleep, recent meditation practice
